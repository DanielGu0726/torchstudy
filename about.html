Our Mission

Democratizing AI Education

TorchStudy was founded with a simple mission: make cutting-edge machine learning knowledge accessible to anyone willing to learn. We believe that the best way to learn AI is through active engagement ‚Äî reading papers, writing code, discussing ideas, and teaching each other.

We are not a class or a lecture series. We are a community of curious minds who are excited about the future of AI and committed to building it together.

History & Milestones

March 2022

A small group of 8 graduate students begin meeting weekly to study PyTorch and deep learning fundamentals.

September 2022

Opened our study sessions to the public, attracting over 40 attendees and growing the community significantly.

Hosted our first symposium with 12 research presentations and 3 invited speakers from industry.
